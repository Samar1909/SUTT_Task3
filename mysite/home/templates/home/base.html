BITS Pilani Library

The BITS Pilani library is housed in a state-of-the-art building, covering about 65000 sq.ft area and is located close to all academic blocks of the Institute. The library indeed is an architectural marvel with an attractive palatial interiors high ceiling adequate natural lighting and ventilation with several series of courtyards, with each used for different activities. Inside the library, there is layout of natural green plants that are soothing for the eyes a seating capacity for 950 student
